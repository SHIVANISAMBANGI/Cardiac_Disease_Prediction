Cardiac Health Assessment Questionnaire

Basic Information

1) What is your age? (in years)

2) What is your biological sex?

Medical Information

3) What type of chest pain do you experience?

4) What is your resting blood pressure? (in mm Hg)

Example: 120

5) What is your total cholesterol level? (in mg/dl)

Refer to your recent blood test report

6) Is your fasting blood sugar higher than 120 mg/dl?

7) What is your resting ECG result?

8) What is your maximum heart rate achieved during exercise?

9) Do you experience chest pain (angina) during physical activity?

10) ST segment depression during exercise, if known

Leave blank if unknown. Example: 1.2

11) Slope of the peak exercise ST segment

12) Number of major blood vessels colored by fluoroscopy (0–3)

13) Thalassemia status (if known)

Symptoms & Lifestyle

14) Do you experience chest pain?

15) Do you have shortness of breath?

16) Do you often feel tired or fatigued?

17) Have you felt your heart skipping or racing (palpitations)?

18) Do you often feel dizzy?

19) Have you noticed swelling in your legs, ankles, or feet?

20) Do you have pain in your arms, jaw, or back?

21) Do you get cold sweats or nausea suddenly?

22) Have you been diagnosed with high blood pressure?

23) Have you been diagnosed with high cholesterol?

24) Are you diabetic?

25) Do you smoke or have a history of smoking?

26) Is your BMI ≥ 30 (obese)?

27) Do you live a sedentary lifestyle (little to no physical activity)?

28) Do you have a family history of heart disease?

29) Are you under chronic stress?
